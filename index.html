About

Bridging strategy and engineering since 2005

I've spent 15+ years at the intersection of technology strategy and delivery — from writing my first web services at Softtek in Monterrey, Mexico, to leading cloud and AI architecture engagements for Fortune 500 banks and insurance companies.

What sets my work apart is that I bridge the gap between executive vision and engineering reality. I've held roles on both sides: as a hands-on senior engineer who led Lowe's first cloud-native application on Microsoft Azure, and as a Senior Solutions Architect at AWS advising enterprises on their most complex cloud challenges.

Today I work directly with clients to design technology solutions that address complex business challenges — from agentic AI platforms that automate customer operations, to security architectures that eliminate critical vulnerabilities. I bring both the technical depth to get the details right and the strategic perspective to ensure the work drives real business value.
